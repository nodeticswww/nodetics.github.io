Cookiebro - Cookie Manager [WebExtension]

Manage your browser cookies to prevent e.g. advertisers from tracking you

  • Configurable automatic deletion of unwanted cookies periodically (even every minute)
  • Cookie whitelist - define which domains can set cookies
  • Cookie blacklist - define domains from which cookies are dropped and not saved at all
  • Easy to use interface - click the extension icon to add a site or domain wildcard to the whitelist
  • Drop unwanted cookies with two clicks at any time
  • Optionally protect session cookies to prevent accidental logout from currently open sites
  • Optionally drop HTTP Response header ETag and Link from blacklisted sites to prevent tracking
  • Optionally delete indexedDB and localStorage caches from websites at browser startup
  • Optionally delete unwanted cookies at browser startup
